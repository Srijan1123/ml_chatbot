## General Admission Requirements
Students applying for undergraduate programs are generally required to:

- Complete higher secondary education (+2 or equivalent)
- Meet minimum academic eligibility criteria
- Submit required academic documents
- Complete admission application procedures

## Required Documents
Typical required documents include:
- Academic transcripts
- Character certificate
- Citizenship or identification documents
- Passport-sized photographs

## Admission Process
1. Submit application form
2. Provide required documents
3. Attend admission counseling or interview if required
4. Complete enrollment procedures
5. Pay admission and academic fees

## Important Notes
Admission requirements may vary depending on the academic program.
