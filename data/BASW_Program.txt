## Program Overview
Bachelor in Social Work (BASW) is designed for students interested in community development, counseling, and social service sectors.

## Core Areas of Study
- Social Work Principles
- Community Development
- Counseling
- Human Behavior
- Social Welfare
- Field Work Practice

## Program Objectives
- Develop social responsibility
- Train professional social workers
- Promote community engagement
- Enhance communication and counseling skills

## Practical Components
- Field visits
- Community projects
- Case studies
- Internship opportunities

## Career Opportunities
Graduates can work in:
- NGOs and INGOs
- Community organizations
- Counseling centers
- Development sectors
- Social service institutions
