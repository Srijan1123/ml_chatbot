## Undergraduate Eligibility

### For BCA
- Completion of +2 or equivalent education
- Interest in computer and technology studies

### For BBS
- Completion of +2 or equivalent education
- Background in management or related studies may be preferred

### For BASW
- Completion of +2 or equivalent education
- Interest in social work and community development

## General Eligibility Notes
- Equivalent qualifications may be accepted
- Students must meet institutional academic standards
