## Overview
Kantipur City College (KCC) is a higher education institution in Nepal offering undergraduate academic programs in management, information technology, social sciences, and related disciplines.

The college focuses on practical education, industry-oriented learning, and academic excellence. KCC provides undergraduate programs affiliated with recognized universities and aims to prepare students for professional careers and higher education opportunities.

## Key Highlights
- Undergraduate-level academic programs
- Industry-focused curriculum
- Practical and project-based learning
- Student-centered teaching methodology
- Academic and career development support
- Extracurricular and co-curricular activities
