1. COLLEGE OVERVIEW
====================================================

Kantipur City College (KCC) is a higher education institution located in Putalisadak, Kathmandu, Nepal. The college focuses on producing skilled human resources capable of meeting modern academic and industrial challenges.

KCC emphasizes:
- Practical-based learning
- Industry-oriented education
- Professional skill development
- Research and innovation
- Student-centered teaching
- Academic excellence

The institution offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in:
- Information Technology
- Engineering
- Business Administration
- Computer Applications
- Artificial Intelligence
- Management

KCC is known for:
- Experienced faculty members
- Practical learning environment
- Workshops and seminars
- Internship opportunities
- Career-oriented education
- Industry exposure

====================================================
2. UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAMS AVAILABLE
====================================================

The undergraduate programs available at KCC include:

1. Bachelor of Computer Application and Information Technology (BCA-IT)
2. Bachelor of Information Technology (BIT)
3. Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)
4. Bachelor of Computer Engineering (BE Computer)
5. Bachelor of Civil Engineering
6. Bachelor of Technology in Artificial Intelligence (B.Tech AI)

Program Duration:
- Most undergraduate programs are 4 years long
- Programs are divided into 8 semesters

7. ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS
====================================================

GENERAL REQUIREMENTS
Applicants must:
- Complete +2 or equivalent education
- Submit required documents
- Pass university entrance examinations
- Attend personal interviews if required

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS
- SEE Character Certificate
- SEE Marksheet
- +2 Transcript
- +2 Character Certificate
- Citizenship Certificate
- Passport-size photographs
- Migration Certificate (if applicable)

====================================================
PROGRAM ELIGIBILITY
====================================================

BCA
- Minimum D grade in each subject in +2
- Aggregate GPA of at least 2.0
- OR minimum 45% in Intermediate Level

BIT
- Minimum D grade in +2
- Aggregate GPA of 2.0
- Mathematics subject required

BBA
- Aggregate D grade in +2
- OR minimum 40% in Intermediate Level

BE Computer
- Minimum C grade in Math, Physics, Chemistry, and English
- OR minimum 45% in Intermediate/Diploma
