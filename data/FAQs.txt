The college offers programs including BCA, BBS, and BASW.

Yes, programs emphasize practical and project-based learning.

Yes, BCA graduates can pursue software development, web development, database administration, and related IT careers.

Yes, students can participate in workshops, seminars, and academic events.

Yes, graduates can pursue postgraduate studies and professional certifications.
