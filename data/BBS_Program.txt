## Program Overview
Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS) is an undergraduate management program focused on business administration, finance, accounting, and organizational management.

## Major Study Areas
- Accounting
- Finance
- Marketing
- Business Communication
- Economics
- Human Resource Management

## Skills Developed
Students develop:
- Leadership skills
- Business communication
- Analytical thinking
- Financial understanding
- Organizational management

## Career Opportunities
Graduates may pursue careers as:
- Accountant
- Business Executive
- Banking Professional
- Marketing Officer
- Administrative Officer
- Entrepreneur

## Educational Objectives
- Develop management capability
- Build professional competence
- Prepare students for business environments
- Enhance decision-making skills
